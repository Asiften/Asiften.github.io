First Technical Post: Starting with Algorithm Analysis

Posted in May 2026

Why begin with analysis?

At the start of an algorithms course, I want to focus on how a solution is described, why it works, and how its cost grows as input size increases.

Three early habits

  • Write the input, output, and key steps clearly.
  • Check correctness on a small example before discussing speed.
  • Use simple asymptotic language to compare alternatives.

Next step

My next posts can build from this foundation by examining one algorithm at a time and recording the main idea, runtime, and limitations.
